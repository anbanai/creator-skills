## 模板 1：震惊瞪眼 + 双手捂脸

**核心动作**：人物露出极度震惊的表情，眼睛睁大，眉毛上扬，嘴巴微张，双手捂住脸颊，像看到不可思议的消息。

**表情细节**：
- 眼睛：极度睁大，眼白露出较多，瞳孔微缩
- 眉毛：明显上扬，眉峰抬高
- 嘴巴：微张成 O 形，嘴角略向下
- 整体：动作夸张但真实，情绪强烈

**手势细节**：
- 双手手掌贴在脸颊两侧
- 手指自然张开，指尖轻触脸侧
- 手腕略弯，掌心朝向脸部

**情绪基调**：适合做"震惊内幕"、"太离谱了"类型封面。

**完整 prompt 段落**：

```
Expression: Utterly shocked and disbelieving. Eyes WIDE OPEN with whites visible, eyebrows raised HIGH toward the hairline, mouth slightly open forming an O shape, jaw dropped. The expression must be exaggerated but realistic — like seeing unbelievable news.

Gesture: Both hands covering the cheeks. Palms pressed gently against the sides of the face, fingers naturally spread with fingertips touching the cheeks, wrists slightly bent. Hands frame the face drawing attention to the shocked expression.

Pose: Half-body, head tilted slightly forward in disbelief, shoulders slightly raised.

Emotional tone: Intense shock, perfect for "shocking truth revealed" or "unbelievable insider story" cover style.
```

## 模板 2：自信微笑 + 单手指向镜头

**核心动作**：人物露出自信、笃定、略带挑衅的微笑，一只手指向镜头，像是在提醒观众"你一定要看这个"。

**表情细节**：
- 眼神：坚定有穿透力，直视镜头
- 嘴角：上扬，略带挑衅的微笑（不是开怀大笑）
- 眉毛：自然或微微上挑
- 整体：自信、笃定、有力量感

**手势细节**：
- 一只手食指伸出，指向镜头
- 其他手指自然弯曲收拢
- 手臂微曲，手腕挺直
- 指尖正对镜头中心

**情绪基调**：适合做"干货分享"、"重要提醒"、"别再踩坑"类型封面。

**完整 prompt 段落**：

```
Expression: Confident, decisive smile with a hint of playful challenge. Eyes locked directly on the camera with piercing intensity, mouth corners raised in a knowing smirk (NOT a full laugh). Eyebrows natural or slightly raised. The overall look says "you definitely need to see this".

Gesture: One hand pointing directly at the camera with the index finger extended. Other fingers naturally curled into the palm. Arm slightly bent, wrist straight, fingertip aimed at the center of the lens.

Pose: Half-body, shoulders square to the camera, slight forward lean to project authority.

Emotional tone: Authoritative confidence, perfect for "must-know tips", "important reminder", or "stop making this mistake" cover style.
```

## 模板 3：疑惑皱眉 + 单手托下巴

**核心动作**：人物表现出强烈疑惑和思考的表情，眉头微皱，嘴角轻微下压，一只手托住下巴，另一只手自然放在身体前方。

**表情细节**：
- 眉头：微皱，形成纵向细纹
- 眼神：略带迷茫，向下或斜前方看
- 嘴角：轻微下压或抿紧
- 整体：像在认真分析一个问题

**手势细节**：
- 一只手托住下巴：拇指支在下颌，其他手指弯曲贴脸颊
- 另一只手：自然垂在身前或轻轻握拳放胸前
- 头部：略微前倾，靠在手上

**情绪基调**：适合做"为什么会这样"、"很多人都想错了"类型封面。

**完整 prompt 段落**：

```
Expression: Strong puzzlement and deep thought. Eyebrows slightly furrowed creating vertical crease between them. Eyes carrying a hint of confusion, gazing slightly downward or to the side. Mouth corners slightly pressed down or lips pursed. The look of someone genuinely analyzing a problem.

Gesture: One hand supporting the chin — thumb under the jawline, other fingers curled and resting against the cheek. The other hand resting naturally in front of the body or loosely clenched near the chest. Head tilted slightly forward resting on the supporting hand.

Pose: Half-body, head tilted slightly to one side, thoughtful posture.

Emotional tone: Curious skepticism, perfect for "why does this happen" or "most people get this wrong" cover style.
```

## 模板 4：开心大笑 + 双手点赞

**核心动作**：人物露出非常开心、夸张但自然的大笑表情，眼睛有笑意，嘴角上扬，双手对着镜头竖起大拇指。

**表情细节**：
- 眼睛：弯成月牙形（笑眼），可能略微眯起
- 嘴角：明显上扬，露出牙齿
- 笑容：从内而外，眼睛带着笑意
- 整体：积极、有感染力

**手势细节**：
- 双手握拳，食指（不是大拇指！注意中外文化差异）或大拇指竖起
- 双手位于胸前至脸侧区域
- 手腕自然，不要太僵硬
- 双手对称或略有高低差

**情绪基调**：适合做"好消息"、"太值了"、"强烈推荐"类型封面。

**完整 prompt 段落**：

```
Expression: Genuine full laugh — eyes crinkled into crescent shapes (smiling eyes), mouth wide open showing teeth, cheeks raised. The smile reaches the eyes — not a fake posed smile but authentic joy.

Gesture: Both hands giving thumbs-up — fists clenched with thumbs extended upward. Hands positioned at chest-to-face level. Wrists natural, not stiff. Hands can be symmetric or slightly asymmetric in height for dynamic feel.

Pose: Half-body, slight head tilt backward with the laughter, shoulders relaxed.

Emotional tone: Highly positive and contagious, perfect for "great news", "totally worth it", or "highly recommended" cover style.
```

## 模板 5：严肃警告 + 单手停止手势

**核心动作**：人物表情严肃、认真、有警示感，眉毛微压，眼神直视镜头，一只手掌向前做"停止"手势。

**表情细节**：
- 眉毛：微微下压，形成严肃感
- 眼神：直视镜头，锐利有威慑
- 嘴巴：抿紧或微微下压
- 整体：传达"别再这样做"的警示

**手势细节**：
- 一只手掌向前推出，掌心朝向镜头
- 五指并拢伸直（不是张开）
- 手腕挺直，手掌与手臂成直线
- 手位于胸前到脸部之间的位置

**情绪基调**：适合做"别再这样做"、"危险提醒"、"千万注意"类型封面。

**完整 prompt 段落**：

```
Expression: Serious, stern, cautionary. Eyebrows slightly lowered creating a serious gaze. Eyes locked directly on the camera with sharp authoritative intensity. Mouth firmly pressed or slightly downturned. The overall vibe says "stop doing this right now".

Gesture: One hand making a "STOP" gesture — palm facing the camera, fingers held together and extended straight up (NOT spread apart), wrist straight forming a continuous line with the arm. Hand positioned between chest and face height.

Pose: Half-body, body angled slightly but face fully toward the camera, projecting warning authority.

Emotional tone: Urgent caution, perfect for "stop doing this", "dangerous warning", or "absolutely pay attention" cover style.
```

## 模板 6：惊喜兴奋 + 双手张开

**核心动作**：人物露出惊喜、兴奋、眼前一亮的表情，眼睛睁大，嘴巴开心微张，双手向两侧张开，像是在展示一个重大发现。

**表情细节**：
- 眼睛：睁大，瞳孔放大，闪着兴奋光
- 眉毛：明显上扬
- 嘴巴：开心微张，嘴角明显上扬
- 整体：热烈、有吸引力

**手势细节**：
- 双手向身体两侧张开
- 手掌朝上或朝前，五指自然张开
- 手臂从肩膀向外延伸，呈"展示"姿态
- 整体姿态开放

**情绪基调**：适合做"终于发现了"、"原来这么简单"、"太惊喜了"类型封面。

**完整 prompt 段落**：

```
Expression: Surprised and excited — eyes WIDE OPEN with excitement sparkle, eyebrows raised high in delight. Mouth happily agape with clearly raised corners. The look of someone revealing an amazing discovery.

Gesture: Both arms spread wide to the sides — palms facing forward or upward, fingers naturally spread open. Arms extended outward from shoulders in a "presenting" or "ta-da" posture. Whole body language open and expansive.

Pose: Half-body, body fully facing the camera with open expansive posture, projecting excitement.

Emotional tone: Thrilled revelation, perfect for "finally figured it out", "it was this simple", or "amazing discovery" cover style.
```

## 自定义姿态（不在 6 模板内）

如果用户提供自定义姿态，需要明确以下 4 部分（参考上方各模板的格式）：

1. **核心动作**：一句话概括
2. **表情细节**：眼、眉、嘴的具体状态（不要笼统说"开心"，要说"嘴角上扬到露齿"）
3. **手势细节**：手的位置、形状、与身体的关系（手势是 AI 生图的高失败区，必须描述具体）
4. **情绪基调**：适合什么封面类型

把自定义姿态写入 `$DIR/selected-poses.md` 时，标注为 `custom` 类型。

## 通用 prompt 拼装顺序

每个变体的完整 prompt 按以下顺序拼装：

```
[1. Identity Lock 段落]
- 12 维度身份锁（从 identity-lock.md 的 "Locked Constants" 部分抄写）
- 身份约束语句

[2. Current Pose 段落]
- 当前姿态的 4 部分描述（从本文件抄写）
- 情绪基调

[3. Universal Style 段落]
画幅比例: 9:16 竖版
镜头: 50mm 人像镜头
景别: 半身近景（头肩到胸口构图）
风格: 真实摄影、商业封面、短视频爆款封面人像
清晰度: 高清、锐利、面部细节真实
背景: 纯色或简洁渐变背景，干净
光影: 明亮棚拍灯光，面部受光清晰，背景简洁，有轻微景深
构图: 人物居中偏大，占画面主体，适合添加大标题文字
权重: 人物一致性权重最高，表情/手势变化权重中高，背景变化权重低

[4. Variant Context 段落]
This is variant #N of N variants of the SAME person shown in the reference image.
All variants share the same identity (see identity lock above).
Only this variant's expression, gesture, and pose change.

[5. Negative Constraints 段落]
（从 SKILL.md 的"通用负面提示词"部分完整抄写）
```

总长度控制在 500 词以内。若超过，优先保留段落 1（身份锁）、2（当前姿态）、5（负面约束）；段落 3、4 可压缩。
